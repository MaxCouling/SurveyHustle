Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 13/10/2024

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in compliance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and our use of Local Differential Privacy (LDP) to further enhance security and privacy.

1. Who We Are

We are SurveyHustle, a survey data collection platform. Our services allow users to create, distribute, and analyze surveys. We collect, process, and store personal data to provide these services securely, incorporating advanced privacy technologies such as Local Differential Privacy (LDP).

2. What Information We Collect

  • Survey Respondents: Information you provide while answering surveys, which may include demographic information, opinions, and preferences.
  • Survey Creators: Information such as name, email address, billing details, and account data.
  • Website Visitors: Information collected via cookies, such as IP addresses, browser types, and usage data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the platform.
  • To personalize user experiences and improve our services.
  • To process payments (if applicable).
  • To communicate with you about your account, surveys, or updates.
  • To analyze data for statistical purposes, in an aggregated and anonymized form.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Legal Bases for Processing Personal Data (GDPR Compliance)

Under GDPR, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent to collect and use your data (e.g., when completing a survey).
  • Contract: Where processing is necessary for the performance of a contract (e.g., for survey creators).
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and safeguard our platform.
  • Legal Obligations: When required to comply with a legal obligation.

5. How We Protect Your Information

We take data security seriously and implement several layers of protection, including:

  • Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest.
  • Local Differential Privacy (LDP): We use LDP techniques to ensure that individual data points remain private. LDP introduces statistical "noise" to responses, allowing us to gather useful insights without compromising the privacy of individual users.
  • Access Controls: We use strict access controls to ensure that only authorized personnel can view or process personal data.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., hosting services, payment processors).
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Survey Creators: If you are a survey respondent, your answers will be shared with the survey creator, but without identifying information unless explicitly agreed upon.

7. International Data Transfers

For GDPR compliance, any transfer of personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA) is done with adequate safeguards in place, such as standard contractual clauses, to protect your data.

8. Your Rights (GDPR and NZ Privacy Act)

You have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing of your data, such as direct marketing.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@surveyhustle.tech.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Survey data is anonymized after one year or deleted upon request by the survey creator or respondent.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website. You can control your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

11. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities within 72 hours, as required by GDPR and the NZ Privacy Act.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and significant changes will be communicated via email or through our platform.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data protection rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@surveyhustle.tech

How to Upload Your Survey

CSV Format

Your CSV file should follow this format:

question_text,question_type,choices
"What is your age?",number,
"Did you know you can change SurveyHustle's theme in Settings?",multiple_choice,"[""Yes"", ""No""]"
"Did you know you can exit the Survey at anytime, and come back to it at any time you feel?",multiple_choice,"[""Yes"", ""No""]"
"How satisfied are you with our service? (Out of 10)",rating,
"Please provide additional feedback.",text,
"How likely are you to recommend us to a friend?",multiple_choice,"[""Very Unlikely"", ""Unlikely"", ""Neutral"", ""Likely"", ""Very Likely""]"

Instructions

  1. Ensure your CSV file follows the format shown above.
  2. The first row should contain the column headers: question_text, question_type, choices.
  3. Each subsequent row represents a question in your survey.
  4. For multiple_choice questions, provide the choices as a JSON-formatted list in the choices column.
  5. For other question types (number, rating, text), leave the choices column empty.
